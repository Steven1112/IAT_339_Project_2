About Our Story

We are here for your cats anytime


Our story inspired by four cats owners, they have amazing stories with their cats. I met the first cat owner when I was in university, I was amazed by her story and the new idea how to identify "your cat". Later, I met the rest of three owners one by one, also, their story is worth to tell and be recorded. After I launched this story, I decided to let more and more people feel these warm stories what I met in my life. Getting along with your cats, we are here to provide food, clothes and toys for them. These things are not just normal stuff, there are defined by something enable to represent your love.


New more stories opening soon


We invited you to join us for the new more stories opening ceremony. We expect more stories between you and your cats, it is worth to tell and share.
