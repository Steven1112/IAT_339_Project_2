About Our Story

We are here for your cats anytime.

"Our story inspired by two cats owners, they have amazing stories with their cats. I met the first cat owner when I was in university, I was amazed by her story and the new idea how to identify "pet" at home. After I launched this story, I decided to let more and more people feel these warm stories what I met in my life. Getting along with your cats, we are here to provide food, clothes and toys for them. These things are not just normal stuff, there are defined by something enable to represent your love. " - CHRISTOPER WILSON (MAIN DIRECTOR)
